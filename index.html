PERSONAL STATEMENT

As a student programmer by training, I discovered my passion for user interface design and user experience while going through various types of courses in college. Having gone through two courses in game design and human-computer interaction had exposed me to the world of design, and its importance in creating products that are user-centered. My goal by the end of my undergraduate journey is to be able to combine both code and design together well, creating the most optimal user experiences.

In my spare time, I try to complete side projects and tutorials in front-end related skillsets. For example, recently I completed a tutorial in converting a Photoshop design to a HTML template, with a different touch through external libraries. I have recently taken up the challenge to pick up AngularJS on the go through online tutorials as well.

Past experiences in schoolwork and projects have led to my understanding that I take more time to process and understand assignments which have a heavier weightage in algorithmic thinking. Although such thinking does not come naturally to me, I am willing to spend more time finding the solutions to the problems, and even if I do not manage to do so, they are valuable lessons for me in developing a more rigorous approach to problem solving continuously.
